Frequently Asked Questions

What should I do if I have lost a valuable item at school?
If you have lost something valuable or an item containing personal data at MU(e.g.USB,wallet,octopus card,mobile phone and computer,etc.), please complete the Report Form and submit it to the security control rooms.
What is the procedure for claiming found items?
For any found or claimed properties, please visit the security control rooms. The staff will asist you in verifying ownership and retrieving your item.
How can I claim sundry supplies items?
Claims for sundry supplies items(e.g. umbrella, water bottle, stationery and glasses,etc.) will be at the self-service corners near the security control rooms on MC, JCC and IOH.
How long will lost items be kept?
Lost items will be retained for a period of one month. Any items that remain unclaimed after this period will be disposed of in suitable ways.
